# Why Canadian businesses still need a website

A website is still the place where a Canadian professional or company can explain who they are, what they do, and how they support clients without depending on a platform they do not own.

## What a website does

- Presents the offer clearly.
- Gives search engines a crawlable source of truth.
- Supports sales, service, and updates.
- Holds the content that social platforms cannot control.

## What AI cannot do for you

AI can help draft, but it cannot replace the judgment required to manage a site, keep it updated, or connect it to your business operations.

## FAQ

### Can a social profile replace a website?

No. Social media is useful, but it is not a substitute for an owned web presence.

## Final takeaway

For Canadian businesses, a website is still the foundation for credibility, support, and growth.
