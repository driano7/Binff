# Social media sales strategies that work in Canada

Social media does not close every sale on its own. It opens the door, builds trust, and sends people to the page where they can make a decision.

## What works

- Share proof instead of generic tips.
- Use short posts to start the conversation.
- Reply fast when someone shows interest.
- Send people to a page with one clear next step.

## What Canadian brands need

Canadian audiences often want credibility before commitment. That means your social content should feel useful, grounded, and supported by a site that explains the offer clearly.

## Why the website still matters

Social platforms change. Your website is the place where the message, the proof, and the contact path stay under your control.

## FAQ

### Which platforms work best for service brands?

LinkedIn and Instagram are usually the best starting points.

### What should a post do?

It should start interest, not try to explain everything at once.

## Final takeaway

In Canada, social media works best when it is part of a larger sales system instead of a standalone tactic.
