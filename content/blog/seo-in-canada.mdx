# SEO in Canada: what it is and why it still matters

SEO in Canada is not only about ranking for one keyword. It is about making your business easy to understand in English and French, and easy to trust in the city or province you actually serve.

## Why it matters here

Canadian buyers compare you against local competitors, large brands, and AI-generated summaries. A clear site still wins when it answers three questions fast: what you do, where you work, and why people should trust you.

## What the site needs

- A clear page structure.
- Service pages with local intent.
- Fast mobile performance.
- Trust signals like reviews and proof.
