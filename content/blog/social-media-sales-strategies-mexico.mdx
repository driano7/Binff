# Social media sales strategies that work in Mexico

Social media can create demand fast, but it converts better when the business has a clear answer ready on the site and in the inbox.

## What works

- Show proof instead of empty posts.
- Reply quickly when someone writes.
- Use short content to start the conversation.
- Send people to one clear landing page or contact path.

## What Mexican brands need

In Mexico, people often respond better when the message feels direct, useful, and easy to continue on WhatsApp or a simple form.

## Why the website still matters

Social channels are useful, but the website is where the offer stays under your control and the lead can be handled properly.

## FAQ

### Which platforms work best?

Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp are usually the most useful starting points.

### What should a post do?

It should earn attention and point to a next step.

## Final takeaway

In Mexico, social selling works best when it is backed by a site and a follow-up process.
